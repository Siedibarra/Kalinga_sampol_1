Our Story

"A partner you can trust"

'Kalinga' is a Filipino word which means protection or to protect in English. We want to protect those who are dearest and closest to us. Kalinga Care Services was founded by two Filipino nurses with over 20 years of experience as frontline practitioners.

We at Kalinga are passionate about quality, safety and patient-centred care. We believe people's choices are crucial to every individual's health and fulfilling life.

Kalinga Care Services is a partner you can trust to deliver and provide the right people and services you, your family or your organisation needs. We understand the plight of our patients and colleagues (nurses and healthcare assistants)," the unsung heroes of the world". That is why we strive to deliver the service with value. We only want the best experience for our clients, nurses, and healthcare assistants.

Therefore, WE provide what we promised.



Our Commitment

"We set high standards for ourselves and work hard to meet them."

A set of morals and principles strictly guides our work. Our work culture revolves around the idea of striving for excellence. What we promise to do, we will do well.

We work with people; thus, we are committed to finding the best talent for our clients and assisting our candidates in reaching their full potential.
